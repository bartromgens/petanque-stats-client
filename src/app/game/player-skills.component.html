TrueSkill Player Rank
Player rank is conservative: it is 99% sure that the player's skill is actually higher. New players have a high skill uncertainty and start with a low rank (0).
TrueSkill Player Skill
Skill is the perceived skill. The bounds indicate the uncertainty. More games will decrease uncertainty.
TrueSkill Player Skill Uncertainty (Standard Deviation)
Player skill uncertainty decreases with more games. A lower uncertainty increases player rank.
